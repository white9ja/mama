Thank you for expressing interest to serve / work as a caregiver

Please note that the following is required to serve as a Caregiver with  Mamà

  1. You must abide by the rules, regulations & safety procedure of Mamà

  2. You must be minimum of 21 years

  3. You must provide a Valid ID Card (National ID Card, Voters Card, Drivers Licence)

  4. You must provide 2 guarrantors (Civil servant, Military Personel or well know / reputable member of the society)

  5. Minimum Education requirement SSCE / WAEC / NECO

  6. You will be called upon or assigned to a government hospital / medical labouratory to conduct medical check up(test)
